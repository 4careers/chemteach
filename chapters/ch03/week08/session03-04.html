THE GENESIS OF PERIODIC CLASSIFICATION

Topics covered today:
3.1-3.2 Genesis of Periodic Classification

Session Summary

  • 🔹 Dobereiner's Triads: Grouped elements in threes with similar chemical properties.
  • 🔹 Newlands' Octaves: Observed properties repeating every eighth element, like musical notes.
  • 🔹 Lothar Meyer's Curve: Plotted atomic volume vs. atomic weight to visually prove periodicity.
  • 🔹 Mendeleev's Periodic Law: Formalized that properties are a periodic function of atomic weight.
  • 🔹 Bold Predictions: Mendeleev left gaps for Eka-Aluminium and Eka-Silicon, predicting their exact properties years before discovery!
